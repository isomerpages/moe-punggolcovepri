2017 has yet been another year full of memories for our two levels of students.  
  
On the last day of school, students gave their best in their class music presentations and reminisced the loving memories they have forged together in 2017 before they were brought to visit their new classrooms with their classmates.  
  
 They used their hands to clean up the classrooms in preparation for 2018. The day ended on a high with students singing the school song with pride and passion. We congratulate our students for achieving yet another milestone in 2017 and moving on to the next level in 2018.
