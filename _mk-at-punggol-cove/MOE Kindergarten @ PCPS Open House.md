<h4>You can register your child for the 2026 Kindergarten 1 (K1) admission to an MOE Kindergarten (MK) if your child is:</h4>
<h4>A Singapore Citizen (SC) or Permanent Resident (PR); and</h4>
<h4>Born between 2 January 2021 and 1 January 2022, both dates inclusive.</h4>
<h4><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/register" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/register</a></h4>
<p></p>
<h4>The online registration for 2026 K1 admission is from 7 to 17 February 2025.</h4>
