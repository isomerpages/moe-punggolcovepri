<p>Our school's Chinese New Year celebration was a delightful cultural immersion,
fostering curious learners and a deeper rootedness to Singapore through
the exploration of Chinese traditions. From vibrant decorations around
the school to engaging activities like blow painting and classroom calligraphy
lesson, it was a week filled with learning and excitement! The highlight
was when the school came together for the captivating assembly programme.
This celebration reminded us of the importance of diversity and inclusion
in our school community!🏮</p>
